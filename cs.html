COMPUTER SCIENCE BASICS

WHAT IS COMPUTER SCIENCE?

Computer Science is the study of Computers and how they work. It involves programming, problem solving, and developing software. Computer Science is used to create applications and technologies that help people in daily life.


AREAS OF COMPUTER SCIENCE

  • Programming
  • Software Development
  • Computer Networks
  • Database Systems

USES OF COMPUTER SCIENCE

  • Education and online learning
  • Business and office work
  • Healthcare systems
  • Communication and social media

ADVANTAGES OF COMPUTER SCIENCE

  • Makes work faster and easier
  • Improves communication
  • Helps in data storage and processing
  • Creates job opportunities

IMPORTANCE OF COMPUTER SCIENCE

Computer Science is important because it is used in education, business, healthcare, and communication. It helps in automation, data processing, and innovation.
